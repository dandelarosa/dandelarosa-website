About

Hello! I am a software engineer with over ten years of experience.

I currently work for the advertising company Publicis Groupe, contributing to the Epsilon PeopleCloud series of products. I write applications using Node and Angular, and I have extensive experience with AWS services including EC2, Lambda, S3, and CloudFront.

Previously, I was a founding engineer at a startup called Makr, best known for the iOS app of the same name. Makr was named Best of 2014 by Apple, nominated for a Webby Award, and purchased in an acquisition by Staples. Before that, I had a hand in developing other mobile applications including Martha Stewart CraftStudio and The Oregon Trail: American Settler.

I am open to discussing next steps. Feel free to contact me on the channels listed below.
